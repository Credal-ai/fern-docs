# Microsoft Teams
The Microsoft Teams connector allows you to send messages to Teams chats and channels efficiently.

## Authentication Setup

## Supported Actions

### Send a Message to a Teams Chat
**Description:** Send a message to a specific Teams chat to communicate directly with individuals or groups.  
**Recommendation:** Ensure the message is clear and relevant to the chat participants for effective communication.

### Send a Message to a Teams Channel
**Description:** Send a message to a Teams channel to share information with a broader audience within a team.  
**Recommendation:** Tailor the message content to the channel's purpose and audience for maximum engagement.
