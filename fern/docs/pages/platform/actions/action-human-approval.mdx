## Double checking action inputs

In Credal, you can enable human approval for any action to ensure that action inputs are reviewed before execution. When human approval is turned on, you'll always be prompted to double-check an action's inputs to make sure they look correct before submitting. This provides an additional safety layer to prevent unintended operations. For control over what the AI can and can't fill in on its own, see [Action Parameters](/user-guide/platform/governed-actions/action-parameters).

Additionally, administrators can enforce human approval at the organization level for specific actions through the System Connectors admin configuration panel. This ensures consistent governance and review processes across your entire organization for sensitive or critical actions.
